Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining

How do I start with Inuka by Kiki?

Starting is easy! Simply choose one of our 5 starter kit options (ranging from R600 to R6000), submit your registration details, and make payment. Once payment is confirmed, your account will be activated and you'll receive your starter kit with all the materials needed to begin your journey.

Which starter kit should I choose?

It depends on your budget and goals. The R600 kit is perfect for beginners, while the R1800 Premium Kit (our most popular) offers the best value. Higher tier kits include more products, better resources, and enhanced support. You can always upgrade later as your business grows.

How long does delivery take?

Once your payment is confirmed, your starter kit is typically dispatched within 24-48 hours. Delivery time varies by location but usually takes 3-5 business days. You'll receive a tracking number to monitor your shipment.

Can I order more products after joining?

Yes! Once you've purchased a starter kit and your account is active, you can order additional products at member prices. This allows you to expand your inventory and grow your business.

What support do I receive as a member?

You'll receive comprehensive support including training materials, marketing resources, access to our community, personal mentorship (on higher tiers), and 24/7 customer support. We're committed to your success!

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your starter kit, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. We stand behind the quality of our products.

How much can I earn?

Earning potential varies based on your effort, dedication, and sales. Many members recover their initial investment within the first month. With consistent effort, members can build substantial income streams. Success stories in our community show earnings ranging from supplemental income to full-time business revenue.

Are the fragrances authentic Inuka products?

Absolutely! All our products are 100% authentic Inuka by Kiki fragrances. We guarantee quality and authenticity on every product. Each item comes with proper certification and meets the highest quality standards.
